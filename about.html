World of Nothing

I stand as the Superior of the In-Between, leader of Organization XIII and ruler of The World That Never Was. As the Superior, I hold dominion over all other Nobodies.

I feel...the emptiness where my companions once stood. I took them for granted. And now, I have...nothing. My first surge of emotion in years...for as long as I can remember...and it's...loneliness.
